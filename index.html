01 / Causal Inference

Minimum Wage & Employment: Card & Krueger Replication

Replicated Card & Krueger's landmark 1994 study using Difference-in-Differences on 410 New Jersey and Pennsylvania fast food restaurants. Estimated the causal effect of NJ's minimum wage hike on FTE employment with HC1 robust standard errors.

DiD estimate: +2.845 FTE (p < 0.05)
